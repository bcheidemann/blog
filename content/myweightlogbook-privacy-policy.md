# My Weight Logbook - Privacy Policy

_Effective date: 29 August 2025_

This policy explains how your information is handled when you use My Weight Logbook (“the App”).

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data. All information generated by the App remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or processed, there are no rights to exercise under data protection laws in relation to the App. If you contact us directly (for example, by email), any information you provide will only be used to respond to your enquiry and will not be shared without your consent.
